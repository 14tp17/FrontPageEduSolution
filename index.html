LumièreVR is experimenting with different ways to film in Virtual Reality using GoPro Rigs and our own stitching technology. We believe every genre of film can be adapted into VR. Our Story

Where we are and where we're going!

Setting

LumièreVR will start beta testing in Las Vegas, Nevada. We aim to contribute to the downtown project by expanding the already booming entertainment and tech scene in downtown vegas. We believe the foottraffic and culture in Vegas is exactly what we need to grow organically.

Rising Action

Although our content will mainly be produced inhouse and distributed via pop up theaters at the beginning, we are aiming for user generated content to fuel our platforms and theaters in the future. This means anyone can submit scripts and stories with top voted content produced into VR films.

Climax

Experience seamless and immersive Virtual Reality with our theater rooms. With sensors at every corner, the entire room can be plugged into our virtual content, allowing you to explore the virtual world with no fiction. We believe the future of film to be interactive rather than passive.
