Jinxy Jenkins, Lucky Lou

Ringling College students Michael Bidinger and Michelle Kwon create this lighthearted short romantic comedy. Now with VR you can live the adventures of either Jenkins or lou in first person!

Lifted

A short film shot on the Ronin-M bringing out the best of your emotions. A VR film of the same genre would allow complete immersion of your surrounding allowing for a wider range of creativity in film techniques.

Run With Me

This short film by Cameron Covell follows Mat, a boy with cerebal, as he decided to run for his school's track meet. VR empowers you to take the role of Mat's peers and run along with him through this emotional journey.
